Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging allows you to merge branches together while maintaining their histories. Rebasing allows you to
change the base of your branch from one commit to another. They both have a similar effect, but some people prefer
rebasing as it results in a cleaner log history.
